Finite Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04/29/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Finite collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our application and related services.

1. Who We Are

Finite is a financial operating system designed to help users understand what money is actually safe to use. Finite is currently operated by Daniel Galvan as an individual founder. Finite does not currently list a business address.

Contact: finitesupport@gmail.com

2. Current App Availability

Finite is currently available through private/internal TestFlight beta distribution. Public App Store availability is not yet confirmed.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as name, email address, phone number, login credentials, or authentication details
  • App usage and settings
  • Financial information you enter directly into Finite
  • Financial account information received through Plaid when you choose to connect an account
  • Transaction data
  • Balance data
  • Income-related data
  • Liability or credit-related data
  • Consent records related to app usage and account linking
  • Support communications
  • Technical information needed to operate and secure the app

4. Plaid and Connected Financial Accounts

If you choose to connect a financial account, Finite uses Plaid to help connect to your selected financial institution.

When you use Plaid Link, you may authorize Plaid and your financial institution to share selected financial account data with Finite. The data available to Finite depends on the account, institution, permissions, and features you authorize.

Finite may use connected financial account data such as transactions, balances, income-related data, and liability or credit-related data to support app functionality.

Finite does not use Plaid balance alone as a guarantee of money that is safe to spend. Bank-observed data may inform Finite, but user-confirmed financial truth remains distinct from detected or suggested data.

5. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve Finite
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Connect financial accounts with your consent
  • Display financial activity and related app features
  • Support user-confirmed financial workflows
  • Maintain account security
  • Provide customer support
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

6. Analytics and Tracking

Finite does not currently use third-party analytics tracking tools in the private/internal beta.

If analytics, crash reporting, performance monitoring, or similar tools are added later, Finite may use them to operate, secure, debug, or improve the app. Finite should not intentionally send sensitive financial data, Plaid tokens, or raw financial payloads to analytics tools without review.

7. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Finite, such as hosting providers, database providers, authentication providers, support tools, and Plaid.

Finite currently uses Render for backend infrastructure. Additional database, authentication, and hosting providers will be identified as the product architecture is finalized.

We do not sell personal financial data.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, to investigate misuse, or in connection with a business transaction such as merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets.

8. Data Security

Finite uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect user data. These safeguards include access controls, MFA on critical systems, encryption in transit, encryption at rest where supported, secret management practices, and restrictions on production access.

No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but Finite works to reduce risk and protect sensitive information.

9. Data Retention

Finite retains information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, or maintain necessary business records.

Current default retention targets:

  • Support logs: up to 90 days
  • Production logs: up to 30 days where feasible
  • Backups: up to 30 days or until provider backup expiration/rotation completes
  • Deletion request completion: within 30 days where feasible, subject to backup cycles and legal/security exceptions

10. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

You may delete your account in the app where supported or request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting: finitesupport@gmail.com.

When you request deletion, Finite will delete or de-identify applicable data unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, backup, or legitimate operational reasons.

11. Disconnecting Financial Accounts

You may disconnect a linked financial account in the app.

Disconnecting a financial account stops future data refreshes for that connection. Some previously collected data may remain in Finite according to this Privacy Policy and Finite’s retention practices unless you request deletion.

12. Your Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain personal information. To make a request, contact finitesupport@gmail.com.

13. Children’s Privacy

Finite is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will take reasonable steps to notify users through the app, website, or other appropriate means.

15. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or Finite’s data practices may be sent to: finitesupport@gmail.com.
